Frequently Asked Questions

We have compiled some of the most asked questions which will assist
While using iDeliverd foods web and app platform.

Does iDeliverd Foods offer only food delivery?

We also offer essentials delivery from your favorite local stores.

What kind of restaurants are listed on iDeliverd Food?

We have a diverse selection of food, restaurants and stores while ensuring they all meet our high-quality standards.

What are the delivery fees?

In-app food items are individually priced. Before confirming your order, kindly review your basket and see the total of your chosen items.

How can I get help with my foods delivery?

If you have any problems with your food or grocery delivery, please contact us in-app.

How does iDeliverd Foods work?

iDeliverd Foods is a food delivery app connecting you with the restaurants and stores in your city which is delivered right to your door.

How long do deliveries take?

Delivery time varies on the restaurant or store, the time of day, traffic, inclement weather and the distance to your location. However, we have 60min ETA.

Can I order from multiple restaurants at once?

You can place multiple simultaneous orders through iDeliverd Food app. However, it’s not possible to purchase items from different restaurants or stores in a single same cart.

How do I get updates on the progress of my order?

You can track your order in-app. We'll also send you notifications at every stage of the delivery process, so you’ll always know where your order is.
