Frequently Asked Questions

We have compiled some of the most asked questions which will assist
you renting a car or taking a ride with iDeliverd.

How much do l pay for the rental?

iDeliverd car rental rates start from as low as $0.15 per minute and you only pay for the time you use the car and the km travelled. However you can use the car as long as you wish

How do l unlock the car?

Once you find the car you’d like to rent, simply tap on the button in your iDeliverd RIDE app to unlock the car and start driving!

Where do l find the keys?

Once you unlock the car with your iDeliverd RIDE app, you’ll find the car keys inside the car.

What if l damaged the car?

If you scratch the car, you’ll definitely be charged a fee from your payment method.

Do l need to pay/add fuel?

Rental fee include fuel, insurance and parking charges upon drop off.

What payment methods do you accept?

iDeliverd RIDE are application operated, we only accept debit and credit card or apple pay payments in-app
