Shipping & Delivery

How do I check my order status?

iDeliverd has made it easy to keep track of account and orders. Sign into “ your account” and click on “orders” or use our tracking available on freight. You can also check status in-app for quick commerce or our shipment partner FedEx and USPS through the waybill code shared after shipment confirmation.

Why isn't my package delivered in 5 days since I paid for expedited express?

Kindly understand the 3~5 business days is only for shipping via Expedited Express, which does not include the processing time. Delivery Time doesn’t consists of 2 days processing time (Processing time is the time it takes to get your order ready to leave our warehouses.

I forgot my account

If you’ve forgotten your password, click on “Forgot your password”. We’ll ask you to enter your email account and then immediately send you anew password.

How to change my account/email address

If your original email address was written incorrectly and you don't have access to it, please don't worry. With this misspelled email address and your password, you can still login in your account.

Can I rush my order?

Unfortunately, at this time you are not allowed to rush your order. Orders are fulfillment in a queue. If you require that your order arrives before a specific day or in case you need products urgently, you should reach out to Customer Support.

How to receive the invoice of my order?

We’re sorry that our website don’t have invoice self-download service. However, you may contact customer service to request a invoice of your order.

How do I update my shipping or billing address?

To change and/or update a shipping address or billing address, manage this segment on your address book.

Can I register the account with mobile phone number?

Yes, you can register the account with mobile phone number when signing up for any iDeliverd products.

Can I purchase without registration?

Once you have added items to the shopping cart you will be required to signup before proceeding. This process enable us curb any fraudulent activities.

What if my shipping address is different than my billing address?

During the checkout process, you have the option to enter a different billing address by clicking “Edit” at the "Billing Address" section in checkout. ensure the billing address you enter below matches the name and address associated with the credit card you are using for this purchase. Please note your billing address and shipping address country must be the same.
